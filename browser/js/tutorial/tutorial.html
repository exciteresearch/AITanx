Library of Functions

Example Functions

Function's Result

tankDoThis()

Takes an action such as "shoot", "layMine", of "move" and causes the tank to take that action.

stuck()

Returns true if the tank has been unable to continue on its given path.

completedPath()

Returns true is a path given to the tank has been completed.

followPath()

Commands tank to follow a designated path

turnTurret()

Takes a number(either positive or negative) and turns the tanks's turret that many degrees

wander()

Tells the tanks to move towards a randomly chosen destination unless higher priorities are discovered.

randomDirection()

Chooses a random direction for the tank to move towards

moveToRegion()

Takes an numerical argument (0-8) that tells the tank to move to one of nine sectors on the map

seekPriorities()

Tells the tank to move towards the highest priorities it encounters

setPriority

Takes two arguments, a target and a numeric priority and deignates that target as having the priority.
